about the event

From hype to execution.

Join us on July 23rd for the Agentics Foundation Silicon Valley event, hosted by Reuven Cohen and Jason La Barbera.

This gathering brings together builders, founders, operators, researchers, investors, and enterprise leaders exploring the future of AI and Agentics. As AI continues to move beyond chat interfaces and into infrastructure, workflows, devices, enterprise operations, and real-world automation, Agentics has become one of the most important areas of innovation.

The conversation has shifted from hype to execution — systems that are sovereign, auditable, composable, and built for practical deployment.

Expect an evening of thoughtful discussion, high-signal networking, and real conversations about where AI and Agentics are going next — especially in the places where Silicon Valley is uniquely positioned to lead.
